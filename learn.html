Crypto Currency

A cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that
is secured by cryptography, which makes it nearly impossible to counterfeit or
double-spend. Many cryptocurrencies are decentralized networks based on blockchain technologyâ€”a distributed
ledger enforced by a disparate network of computers. A defining feature of cryptocurrencies is that they
are generally not issued by any central authority, rendering them
theoretically immune to government interference or manipulation.
